How It Works

Tellus is a free property management app designed to take the stress out of managing rentals.

With a mobile platform giving you access wherever you are, you’ll feel like you live next door.

Quick and easy to get started.

1. Choose Your Role

Download the app and choose your role as a tenant, owner, or property manager for a custom onboarding experience.

2. Claim Your Property Address

Search for the property you own, rent, or manage and claim it on the app.

3. Upload Your Lease

If you’re a landlord or manager who wants to collect rent, upload your lease details to get started.

4. Send Invites

Once the lease is ready, landlords and managers can invite their tenants to start paying rent and chatting through Tellus. Tenants can also invite their owner or manager.

Quick and easy to get started.

Go More In-Depth on Our Features

Payment

Never miss a rent payment again. Tellus lets you tackle reimbursements, late fees, and security deposits with confidence.

Documents

Tellus is a living archive of all home related documents. Save leases, property information, receipts, invoices, and inspection reports.

Mobile First

Experience the freedom of managing on the go with the Tellus mobile app. Rest assured that everything is available instantly, without having to be tied to your computer.

Finances

Stay on top of your finances with professional reports and real-time cash flow. Use the Ledger to track expenses with one tap.

Digital Lease

Save hours of time by creating or renewing a lease from your phone. Tenants can sign digitally.

Teamwork

Having a centralized platform through Tellus makes it easy for managers and owners to stay on the same page. Exceptional transparency means you don’t have to file a report.

Communication

Tellus Chat is the most efficient way to communicate with landlords, managers, and tenants. Send notices, alerts, and work orders.

Customer Service

Making your life easier is our job. It’s that simple. Whether you need help with setup or you run into a snag, we are here to help.

Work Orders

Keeping a unit in top shape should be a priority for everyone. See how easy it is to create a work order and find a service professional.

Flexibility

Pick and choose as many or as few features as you would like for full customization. Tenants still want to pay with checks? No problem.

Listings

Write one description and let us post it to 16+ websites for free. Tellus will take care of advertising your rental so that your unit doesn’t stay vacant.

Tenant Screenings

Compare credit reports, rental history, and criminal records for a thorough screening, free to all parties.

Ready to try it out? Download the app and simplify your rental experience.
